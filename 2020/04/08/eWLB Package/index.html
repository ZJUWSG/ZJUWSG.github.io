芯片封装 Package

晶圆级封装主要以BGA(Ball Grid Array)技术为基础，以wager为加工对象，在wager上同时对众多芯片进行封装测试，最后切割成单个期间，可以直接贴合到基板或者PCB上。

FI_WLP VS eWLB/FO_WLP

传统fan-in WLP必须将所有I/O安置在芯片尺寸范围内，而fan-out WLP是一种wager重构技术，突破了芯片尺寸限制，可以使芯片具有更多I/O数，更便宜，是芯片封装的大势所趋

eWLB工艺：

1.将wafer固定
2.将芯片倒扣到wafer上
3.用模塑料对芯片进行空隙填充
4.将wafer和胶带从系统中分离
5.进行RDL和焊球工艺

WLP工艺(2P1M)

1.WLP incoming 封装入料
2.1st polymer layer（1st passivation）
3.RDL 重布线
4.Ball place植球
5.UBM球下金属层
6.2nd polymer layer(2nd passivation)

名词解释

BGA：A ball grid array (BGA) is a type of surface-mount packaging (a chip carrier) used for integrated circuits. BGA packages are used to permanently mount devices such as microprocessors(微处理机). A BGA can provide more interconnection pins than can be put on a dual in-line or flat package. The whole bottom surface of the device can be used, instead of just the perimeter. The traces connecting the package’s leads to the wires or balls which connect the die to package are also on average shorter than with a perimeter-only type, leading to better performance at high speeds.

BGA具有高密度、导热性、低电感引脚的优势，缺点是BGA材料锡球无法像长引脚那样延展，不具材料刚度。所有的表面贴焊装置，因PCB基板和BGA封装在热膨胀系数的差异而弯曲（热应力），或延展并振动（机械应力）下，就可能导致焊点断裂。

TSV: Through Silicon Via.TSV uses only back-end manufacturing techniques such as bonding, fine pitch bumping, back grinding and thin wafer handling.
